What to Do During a Disaster

Earthquake

Drop, Cover, and Hold On. Stay indoors until the shaking stops.

Flood

Move to higher ground and avoid walking through floodwaters.

Fire

Stay low to avoid smoke, use the nearest exit, and never use elevators.

Storm

Stay indoors, away from windows, and have emergency supplies ready.
